Project Overview

This project dives into sales performance using Power BI to uncover trends in revenue, profitability, and loss-making areas across categories, sub-categories, regions, and years. This report explores the customer data  and provide actionable insights that can guide business decisions.

Objectives

Track overall sales and profit performance

Spot top-performing and underperforming sub-categories

Compare sales growth with profitability

Highlight regions that are doing well and those that need attention

 Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop

Excel / CSV (data source)

DAX for calculations and KPIs

 Key Metrics

Total Sales

Total Profit

Profit Margin (%)

Average Order Value

 Key Insights

Technology is the biggest profit driver, while Furniture struggles with low margins.

Tables and Bookcases are the sub-categories causing losses.

Phones and Copiers bring in high profits even with fewer orders.

California and New York lead in sales, but some states aren’t profitable despite good revenue.

Sales saw a major jump after 2015, but profit hasn’t always kept pace.

 Recommendations

Focus on growing high-margin products rather than chasing just volume.

Revisit pricing and discounts for loss-making sub-categories.

Streamline operations and logistics in states with low profitability.

Keep costs in check during periods of rapid sales growth to maintain steady profits.
